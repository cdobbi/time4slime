About 'Time 4 Slime'

Welcome to Time 4 Slime, created by our 12-year-old slime expert! With 4 years of crafting and experimenting with slime, she's developed recipes that surpass the best commercial slimes. Her slime is made with love, perfect for playtime, stress relief, and endless fun. Each individual slime is handcrafted to be fluffy, or gooey, visually and texturally appealing, perfectly satisfying your slime craving.
